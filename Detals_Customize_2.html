Stadium name

Aviation Club Stadium

Location

New Minya next to the Police Club

Description of the stadium

It is a large place where big celebrations are held for
all celebrities. The most important thing that
distinguishes it is that the audience is in a place
far from the person holding the party
to increase safety and prevent any harm from
occurring. It is distinguished by its large
area and large number of people.
